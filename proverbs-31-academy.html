SIX-WEEK FORMATION JOURNEY

Proverbs 31 Academy

This is not about being perfect, performing femininity, or doing more. It is a study in wisdom, dignity, courage, stewardship, service, and a life anchored in God.

Week 1 · A woman of wisdom

Read Proverbs 31:10–12. Study how wisdom begins with reverence for God and shows up in real choices.

Week 2 · Identity & dignity

Read Proverbs 31:25 and Psalm 139. Practice speaking to yourself with truth, compassion, and spiritual confidence.

Week 3 · Stewardship & discipline

Consider time, money, gifts, and energy as things entrusted to you—not measurements of your worth.

Week 4 · Love in action

Explore kindness, boundaries, friendship, and service. Practice one act of care that protects dignity—yours or someone else’s.

Week 5 · Purpose & courage

Read Esther 4. Name the place where you are being called to prepare, speak, or serve with courage.

Week 6 · Building a lasting life

Create your personal rule of life: the simple weekly practices that help you remain close to God and care for your whole self.
