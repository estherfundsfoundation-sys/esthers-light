# Adding content safely - Label direct Bible text with its reference and licensed/public-domain translation. - Keep historical context, interpretation, reflection, and application visibly distinct from Scripture. - Use “traditionally attributed to” for disputed authorship/dating. - Do not promise wealth, healing, academic success, or safety in exchange for faith or donations. - Keep crisis pages connected to 988, 911 for immediate danger, campus support, counseling, healthcare, and trusted people. - Public prayer submissions need moderation, privacy controls, reporting, and a backend before launch.
